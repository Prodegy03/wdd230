Audience

Our audience will include:
1. Individuals who belong to the comunity and wish to know more about where they live.
2. Individuals who are visiting, site-seeing, or vacationing, and wish to experiance all the nooks and crannies of our lovely area.

Personas

Persona 1: Liam Neeson, Actor Name: Liam Neeson Occupation: Actor Demographic: 69 years old. 2 children, fairly high income. Liam’s story: Famous actor from Ireland, sometimes he just needs a secluded place to get away.

Persona 2: George MacNaughtan, Store owner Name: George MacNaughtan Occupation: Buisness Owner Demographic: 72 years old. 4 children. George’s story: Born and raised in Bracebridge, married and had children in Bracebridge. He is the definition of local.

Scenarios

1. Liam is getting away from the hubub of celebrity life. He wants to go to an exclusive place where nobody will really bother him. He sees Bracebridge on a list of vacation places and decides to look for a place he could stay in this out of the way small town.

2. Liam is visiting Bracebridge, but he isn't sure where he would like to go eat. He checks Bracebridge's chamber of commerce website to see what the locals say.

3. Liam decides he wants to take a look at the different views around the area of Bracebridge. To see what the best places to visit he checks Bracebridge chamber of commerce.

4. George wants to know what the city, and people think of his little store. So he googles his location and finds the Chamber of Commerce website for Bracebridge. He is able to see where he rates in a collective viewpoint from his peers.

5. George is trying to get information about events and specials from his store out into the community. So he visits Bracebridge's chamber of commerce website and contacts them to add information to the page showing his buisness.
