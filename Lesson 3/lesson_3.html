Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to give the people in the area or visiting the area of Bracebridge, On Canada an idea of the essential buisnesses, and goals of the buisness comunity. There will be two sectors in this chamber of commerce the private, and the public. The private sector will include all privately owned buisnesses that opt to be a part of this chamber of commerce group. The Pulic sector will include all buisnesses that are ran by the city or goverment.

Audience

Our audience will include: 1. Individuals who belong to the comunity and wish to know more about where they live. 2. Individuals who are visiting, site-seeing, or vacationing, and wish to experiance all the nooks and crannies of our lovely area.
